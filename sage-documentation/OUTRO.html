Wrapping up

Thank you, again, for contributing to and supporting the open source community.

I hope this book has helped you get more familiar with Sage and pushes you to learn new tools to help you improve your development workflow.

Things always change fast in the web world, and Sage will continue to change as both the web and WordPress move forward.

A quote from Don Denoncourt's On Getting Old(er) in Tech I wanted to share:

Treat this year as if it were your first year as a developer and assimilate everything you can. Reclaim the energy you had in your first year of coding. Regain the drive you had to prove to yourself and to your employers that you were “all that” for this IT field. Resume reading about tech, playing with new techniques, and persuading others to teach you. Reacquire the excitement of collaborating on newfound knowledge with other developers. Be a lifelong learner and investigate all forms of learning.

This book will be updated as major changes to Sage are made — you’ll get notified via email when new editions are released. You’ll be able to immediately download new releases for free if you’ve purchased this book through https://roots.io/.

Please write to ben@roots.io with any corrections or feedback and join us at the Roots Discourse (https://discourse.roots.io/) for discussion.
